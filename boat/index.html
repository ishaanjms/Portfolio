boAt Lifestyle

A visual brand-system exploration for boAt that stretches its street-culture energy across campaign media, stationery, and everyday merchandise.

Project Brief

From campaign posters to a complete brand system

This project started as an assignment for my Graphic Design course. I chose boAt Lifestyle because I wanted to go beyond isolated posters and build a full brand system. The challenge was to keep their loud, street-culture energy consistent across a serious corporate letterhead, a mass-media campaign, and merchandise used every day.
